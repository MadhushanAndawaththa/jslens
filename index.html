Privacy

Local-only by construction

Your keywords, colors, and settings live in chrome.storage.local on your device. No accounts, no telemetry, no sync, no remote code. Every file that ships in the extension package is inspectable via chrome://extensions.

0 network calls · 0 analytics SDKs · 0 servers

How it works

Three steps. Then forget it's installed.

Once your keywords are saved, the extension just runs. No dashboards to babysit, no notifications, no daily prompts.

  1. Install

    One-click install from the Chrome Web Store. No account creation, no email confirmation, no onboarding flow.

  2. Open LinkedIn Jobs

    Viewed, saved, and applied cards fade automatically. Company size and LinkedIn employee count appear next to the job title. Save keywords from the popup or right-click any text on the page.

  3. Keep your keywords with you

    Your highlight terms also light up on Indeed, Glassdoor, Seek, and any other site — so the same vocabulary follows you around the web.
